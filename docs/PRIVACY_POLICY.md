# Privacy Policy for Kenya Audit Transparency Platform

**Last Updated: October 1, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Kenya Audit Transparency Platform ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our platform:

- **Log Data**: IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and pages viewed
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with our platform, features you use, and time spent on pages
- **Cookie Data**: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Cookie Policy)

### 2.2 Information You Provide

If you create an account or contact us, we may collect:

- Email address
- Name (optional)
- Any information you provide in communications

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- **Provide Services**: Operate and maintain the platform
- **Improve Services**: Analyze usage patterns and improve functionality
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
- **Legal Compliance**: Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- **Communications**: Send service-related announcements (if you have an account)

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share information:

- **With Service Providers**: Cloud hosting, analytics, and monitoring services
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights
- **Aggregated Data**: We may share anonymized, aggregated data publicly

## 5. Government Data Sources

This platform aggregates publicly available government financial data from:

- National Treasury of Kenya
- Office of the Auditor General (OAG)
- Office of the Controller of Budget (COB)
- County governments

We do not control or own this data. All government data remains the property of the respective government agencies.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Log Data**: Retained for 90 days for security and analytics
- **User Accounts**: Retained until you request deletion
- **Government Data**: Retained indefinitely for historical analysis

## Cookie Policy

### What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide and improve our services.

### Types of Cookies We Use

1. **Essential Cookies**: Required for the platform to function (e.g., session management)
2. **Analytics Cookies**: Help us understand how visitors use the platform (Google Analytics, Sentry)
3. **Performance Cookies**: Monitor platform performance and errors

### Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect functionality.

### Third-Party Cookies

We use:

- **Google Analytics**: Analytics and usage tracking
- **Sentry**: Error monitoring
